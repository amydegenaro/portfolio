About

I majored in environmental studies in college and started my professional career in data entry at a state environmental government agency. I then moved on to a position in publishing for environmental textbooks, where I used databases to maintain product information for 5-6 textbooks a year. Most recently, I was working in the fundraising office for a public park non-profit in Boston, where I was the donor database administrator and also managed the donation forms and pages on our website.

Being in a small non-profit, we didn’t have anyone on staff with direct responsibility for the website, so I took on a lot of the responsibilities out of interest and technical ability. Being able to see behind the scenes on our website and learn how everything worked is what sparked my passion for software engineering, leading me to the Grace Hopper program at Fullstack Academy. I’d love to continue this career path at a mission-based organization in either a back end role working with databases, or on the front end with user experience, since I love seeing my work have a real impact.

Galacticode

Galacticode uses a 3D gaming interface to guide users through increasingly difficult JavaScript challenges. Play as Nova the alien, who is lost in space and needs help getting home after running out of fuel on an unfamiliar planet. Use your foundational JavaScript skills to solve coding challenges, gain fuel points, unlock planets, and guide Nova home.

Parkitech

Parkitech is a geogtagged ticket board for park management that was created to fill a technology gap often found in small non-profits managing public parks and spaces. Maintenance and Park Ranger teams work both in the field and the office and need a good way to track and record tasks or issues from both places at once. Parkitech is a ticket board with a responsive interface for use on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. Tickets are geotagged to record and plot their exact coordinates on a map, allowing for better tracking, organization, and analytics of park operations.

Built With

Node, Express, PostgreSQL, Sequelize, React, Redux, and Mapbox
